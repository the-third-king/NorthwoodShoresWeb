The Lake
DNR Informational Website

The lake has many features including a sand beach and 2 fishing docks.

The lake is populated with Croppie, Northern, and large-mouth bass.

A Wisconsin fishing license is required.

Additionally, the channel connects us with Lower Devils lake.

We also offer a paddle boat rental. Please visit the lodge to see rates.
